The Mechanism

Three receptors,
one molecule.

Retatrutide is a once-weekly peptide that activates the GLP-1, GIP, and Glucagon receptors simultaneously — addressing satiety, insulin response, and energy expenditure in concert. The NEJM 2023 Phase 2 trial reported the largest mean reduction of any single agent in modern obesity research.

#1 · GLP-1
Satiety

Slows gastric emptying and increases fullness via the hypothalamus — the classic mechanism, but only one of three.

#2 · GIP
Insulin

Improves glucose-dependent insulin secretion and modifies fat-cell sensitivity to lipolysis signals.

#3 · Glucagon
Energy

Increases resting energy expenditure and hepatic fat oxidation — the differentiator vs semaglutide.
