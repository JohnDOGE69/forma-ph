Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026 · FORMA Philippines

FORMA Philippines ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

1. What We Collect

We collect only what you voluntarily provide:

  • Waitlist submissions — your phone number or email address when you join the Batch 2 waitlist
  • Calendly bookings — name, email, and any information you provide when booking a consultation call (handled by Calendly's own privacy policy)
  • Usage data — anonymous analytics via Google Analytics and Meta Pixel (page views, session duration, general location). No personally identifiable information is attached to these.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To notify you when a new batch opens (waitlist)
  • To confirm and manage your consultation booking
  • To understand how visitors use the site and improve it
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing

3. Data Storage

Waitlist submissions are stored in Supabase, a cloud database service hosted on AWS infrastructure. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Supabase complies with SOC 2 Type II standards.

4. Retention

We retain waitlist contact information for up to 12 months or until you request deletion. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights (NPC / RA 10173)

Under Philippine law you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous traffic analytics
  • Meta Pixel — anonymous ad performance measurement
  • TikTok Pixel — anonymous ad performance measurement

You may opt out of analytics tracking via your browser's privacy settings or tools like uBlock Origin.

7. Research Disclaimer

FORMA compounds are sold strictly for research and laboratory purposes. By using this site you confirm compliance with all applicable local laws. This site does not constitute medical advice.
